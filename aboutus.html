ABOUT US

Founded in 2010 by Hnin Si, May Hnin Si Family is a national nonprofit organization. Endorsed by the Myanmar Academy of Pediatrics and winner of the prestigious Hnin Si Foundation Award for Publication of the Year, May Hnin Si Family includes a diverse and inclusive collection of products for children, parents, and older adults. In addition to magazines and cookbooks, the May Hnin Si Family brand includes the Eatable Alphabet, digital content, and cooking curricula, reaching over million families worldwide.
